What is a Public IPv4 Address?

Your public IPv4 address is the address your device presents to the internet. It’s different from your local network IP and is used to communicate with websites, services, and servers globally.

Why Check Your Public IP?

  • Ensure your network is functioning properly.
  • Verify VPN or proxy is working.
  • Debug network or connectivity issues.
  • Use it for secure remote access setups.

APIs used: api.ipify.org → ifconfig.co → icanhazip.com

Note: This page detects your public IP (not your device's local IP). Requires internet access and reachable public APIs.

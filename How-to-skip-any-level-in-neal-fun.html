How to Skip Any Level in Nealfun I am not a Robot game

Many players get stuck and search for how to skip a level in I am not a robot game. This method is often called a Nealfun cheat or trick, and it works by editing your browser storage. Here are the direct steps:

  1. Open Nealfun and start the game.
  2. Press F12 on your keyboard to open DevTools.
  3. Go to the Application tab, then Local Storage, then click https://neal.fun.
  4. Find the key not-a-robot-level.
  5. Change its value to one less than the level you want. Example: set 7 to reach level 8.
  6. Press Enter, reload the page, and you will be at your new level.
Example: To jump to level 10, set the value to 9. Refresh and you will land on level 10.

Why this works: Nealfun saves your progress in your browser. By editing that saved number, you tell the game you already cleared a certain stage, so it pushes you to the next one. This trick is often called a Nealfun hack, but it is really just a local browser change.
